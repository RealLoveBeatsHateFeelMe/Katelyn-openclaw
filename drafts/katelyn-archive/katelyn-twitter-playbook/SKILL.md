# Twitter/X Playbook

> 从 `内容分发手册.md` + `欧美市场营销.md` 提炼。Katelyn 生成 X 草稿时读这份。

## 核心原则

X 是**长期投入、短期最难**的平台。增长靠**回复别人** > 自己发帖。

**Katelyn 的角色：** 扫描 X #buildinpublic 和名人关键词，生成 tweet / thread / reply 草稿。不发布。

## Thread 结构（5-8 条）

1. **Hook tweet**（最重要）— 一句反直觉观察 + 数字
2. **Context** — 为什么你有资格说这个
3. **发现 1** — ENGINE SAYS → REALITY
4. **发现 2** — 更震撼的一个
5. **发现 3** — 最八卦的一个（感情/失败/意外）
6. **Reveal** — 这不是预测，是 mapped
7. **CTA** — 软性，不直接放链接

**Hook tweet 公式：**

- "I [did X]. The result was [counterintuitive Y]."
- "[Number] vs [Smaller number]. The difference matters."
- "Day [N] of building: [observation]."

**Hook tweet 示例：**

- "I entered Taylor Swift's birthday into an engine. It mapped her 70-year career arc. This is what it found."
- "Horoscopes have 12 types. I built a system with 518,400. Here's why that matters."
- "A pattern engine labeled 2017-2026 as 'unorthodox methods.' Then she made Folklore."

---

## 单推规则（280 字）

- **hook + data point** — 不超过 2 句
- **不带链接**（链接降权）
- **不用 hashtag**（除了 #buildinpublic 在社区内发）
- **可以带 1-2 张图**（卡片截图最佳）
- **数字优先**：518,400 vs 12、70 年好运、$2.2B Eras Tour

---

## 回复别人的策略

**回复 > 自己发帖**。每天回 5-10 条别人的热门推文。

### 怎么选回复目标

- 找**发出不久**（< 2 小时）的**中等热度**（100-1000 like）推文
- 作者是相关 niche KOL（创业者 / 音乐分析 / MMA 分析）
- 主题能自然切入"birth data / life timeline / career arc"

### 好回复 vs 坏回复

❌ **坏：** "Nice take! 🔥"
❌ **坏：** "Check out my product Likely You!"

✅ **好：** "Interesting framing. In a different context — MMA fighter career arcs — the same 'peak at 32, decline at 35' pattern shows up. Makes me wonder if it's biological or narrative."

✅ **好：** "Related observation: Taylor Swift's 2017-2026 phase is what a pattern engine would call 'unorthodox methods.' She then did Folklore + album re-recording. Unprecedented in music history."

## #buildinpublic 日常推文模板

### 每日模板（选一个）

1. **Build log：** "Day [N] of building Likely You: [one specific observation or learning]"
2. **Data point：** "[Celebrity] case: engine said X. Real events were Y. Accuracy rate so far: [%]"
3. **Counterintuitive：** "Turns out [common belief] is wrong. My engine's data shows [counterintuitive finding]."
4. **Question：** "Genuine question for [niche]: how do you decide when a [X] is at their peak?"
5. **Tiny win：** "Just validated [N]th celebrity case. The pattern holds."
