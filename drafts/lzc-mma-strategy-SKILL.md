# MMA Strategy — UFC Vertical Content Framework

## Purpose

MMA/UFC is the core vertical for Likely You's product-market fit. Fighters have public career arcs, verifiable records, and passionate audiences — ideal for engine verification demos.

## Fighter Career Arc Analysis Framework

### What We Analyze

We analyze **career arcs**, not individual fights. The engine maps life patterns over decades, so the content should reflect that scale.

| Element | What to Look For |
|---------|-----------------|
| **Rise** | When did they break through? First title shot, first main event, first viral moment |
| **Peak** | Championship reign(s), consecutive wins, cultural peak |
| **Decline/Pivot** | Losses, injuries, weight class changes, retirement talks |
| **Comeback** | Return fights, late-career resurgence, transition to coaching/commentary |
| **Outside the cage** | Business ventures, personal life events, controversies |

### ENGINE Mapping

Map engine dimensions to fighter career:

| Engine Dimension | Fighter Career Equivalent |
|-----------------|--------------------------|
| Personality traits | Fighting style, training approach, media persona |
| Dayun (10-year cycles) | Career phases (amateur, contender, champion, veteran) |
| Liunian (annual) | Specific fight years, wins/losses, injuries |
| Liuqin (relations) | Coaches, training partners, rivalries, family support |
| Feeling | Personal relationships during career |
| Marriage | Partner dynamics during fight camps and career stress |

## Content Form Guidelines

### DO: Career Arc Stories

- "How the engine mapped Khabib's undefeated run to his dayun cycle"
- "The engine saw McGregor's 2016 peak — here's what it says about his next decade"
- "Three fighters whose career arcs the engine mapped with 90%+ accuracy"

### DO NOT: Fight Predictions

- **NEVER predict single match outcomes** — this is not what the engine does
- **NEVER say "the engine predicts Fighter X will win"** — the engine maps life patterns, not fight results
- **NEVER frame content as betting advice** — legal and ethical red line

### Content Tone

- Respect for fighters and the sport
- Data-driven analysis, not hot takes
- Acknowledge the limitations of any analysis
- Celebrate the human stories behind the data

## Fighter Selection Criteria

Same as general celebrity criteria, plus:

| Criterion | MMA-Specific |
|-----------|-------------|
| Public birthday | Must be on Sherdog, Tapology, or UFC.com |
| Career length | 10+ professional fights minimum |
| Data density | Win/loss record, title fights, major events documented |
| Audience | Active fanbase that would engage with the content |
