# Product Context — What Likely You Is (and Is Not)

## The Core Thesis

> "We prove we know your past — so you'll believe what we see in your future."

This is the entire product in one sentence. Everything we do ladders up to this.

The engine takes birth data and outputs a life profile. We then verify that profile against documented real-world events. When the engine accurately maps someone's past — career peaks, relationship patterns, personality traits — it earns credibility for what it projects about their future.

## The Numbers

- **518,400 types** — our engine models this many distinct profiles based on birth data
- **12 zodiac signs** — what astrology uses
- That is a 43,200x resolution difference. This is not astrology.

## What We Are

- A **birth data pattern analysis engine**
- A **computational model** with 518,400 profile types
- A **verification-first product** — we prove accuracy before asking for trust
- A **data company** that happens to use birth data as its input signal

## What We Are NOT

- NOT astrology
- NOT fortune telling
- NOT horoscopes
- NOT spiritual guidance
- NOT "woo"
- NOT a prediction service (we map patterns, we do not predict specific events)

The founder (X / Xianzhi) has deep conviction about this distinction. It is not negotiable. If content could be mistaken for astrology, it fails.

## The Three-Card System

The product's visual/UX metaphor uses three cards:

| Card | Engine Dimension | What It Shows |
|------|-----------------|---------------|
| **Personality Card** | `personality.dominant_traits` | Who you are — behavioral profile, dominant traits with percentages |
| **Liunian Card** | `liunian` | Where you are — this year's patterns, current annual cycle |
| **Dayun Card** | `dayun` | Where you're going — current 10-year major cycle phase |

Users get these three cards as their primary product experience. Each card is backed by data, not vibes.

## MMA as Core Vertical

MMA/UFC is the primary vertical for product-market fit because:

1. **Public data density** — fighters have detailed public records (wins, losses, dates, opponents)
2. **Career arc clarity** — rise, peak, decline, comeback are all visible
3. **Passionate audience** — UFC fans are highly engaged and data-literate
4. **Verifiability** — every claim can be checked against Sherdog/Tapology/UFC.com
5. **Content opportunity** — UFC events create natural content windows

## How to Talk About the Product

### In Demos

Lead with verification, not claims:
- "Here's what the engine output for Taylor Swift. Now let's check it against her real career."
- "The engine flagged 2016 as a peak year for McGregor before we looked at his record."

### In Content

Always ground in specifics:
- "The engine mapped 85% of his career milestones to the correct dayun windows."
- "Three of five personality traits matched documented behavior patterns."

### Never

- "The engine predicted she would be successful" (too vague, sounds like fortune telling)
- "According to her birth chart..." (wrong vocabulary)
- "The stars say..." (absolutely not)

## Competitive Positioning

We are not competing with astrology apps (Co-Star, The Pattern). We are competing with:
- Personality assessments (MBTI, Big Five)
- Career analytics tools
- Life pattern analysis

Our unique angle: birth data as a novel input signal for pattern analysis, verified against reality.
